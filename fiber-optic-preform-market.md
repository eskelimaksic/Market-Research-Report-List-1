<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Preform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VAD</li><li>OVD</li><li>PCVD</li><li>MCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic preform market includes several manufacturing techniques: </p><p>1. **VAD (Vapor Axial Deposition)** involves depositing silica layers onto a rotating rod, ideal for high-performance fibers.</p><p>2. **OVD (Outside vapor deposition)** coats a mandrel with silica, allowing for precise control over the fiber structure.</p><p>3. **PCVD (Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition)** utilizes plasma to deposit silica, enhancing material properties.</p><p>4. **MCVD (Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition)** involves an internal process where silica is deposited inside a rotating transparent tube. Each method contributes to the quality and efficiency of optical fibers.</p></p>
