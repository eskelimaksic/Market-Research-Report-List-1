<p><ul><li>CCOT (Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube) System</li><li>TXV (Thermal Expansion Valve) System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air-conditioning system market features several key types, including the Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube (CCOT) system and the Thermal Expansion Valve (TXV) system. The CCOT system utilizes an orifice tube to control refrigerant flow, optimizing performance while being cost-effective and simple in design. In contrast, the TXV system employs a valve to adjust refrigerant flow based on temperature changes, providing enhanced efficiency and comfort. Other market types may include hybrid systems and more advanced electronic controls, catering to diverse vehicle requirements.</p></p>
