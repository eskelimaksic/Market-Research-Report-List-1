<p><p>Video Content Analytics (VCA) software encompasses two primary market types: camera-based systems and server-based systems. Camera-based systems integrate analytics directly within IP cameras, enabling real-time data processing at the source, which reduces latency and network load. In contrast, server-based systems centralize analytics processing on dedicated servers, allowing for more extensive computational power and advanced analytics capabilities, often suitable for large installations. Both systems enhance video surveillance by automating tasks such as motion detection, object recognition, and behavior analysis.</p></p>
