<p><p>HR software solutions are primarily categorized into two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises solutions are installed locally on a company's servers and require substantial upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, delivering greater control and customization. In contrast, cloud-based solutions operate via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. They facilitate remote access and updates, making them suitable for organizations looking for efficient, cost-effective HR management without the need for extensive IT infrastructure.</p></p>
