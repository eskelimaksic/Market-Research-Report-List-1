<p><p>The Invisible Car Cover market is categorized into three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) covers are cost-effective and provide basic protection but may lack durability. PU (Polyurethane) covers offer better flexibility and resistance to UV rays, making them more suitable for long-term use. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) covers combine the benefits of both PVC and PU, offering superior durability, stretchability, and transparency. These varying materials cater to different consumer needs regarding protection, longevity, and budget.</p></p>
