<p><p>The Polymer Material Light Stabilizer market encompasses various types designed to enhance the durability and longevity of materials exposed to light. Free radical scavengers prevent degradation by neutralizing harmful free radicals generated by UV exposure. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation, converting it into harmless energy. Quenchers dissipate energy from excited states, reducing the risk of photodegradation. Other stabilizers include inhibitors and antioxidants that collectively protect polymers from fading, discoloration, and mechanical failures due to environmental factors.</p></p>
