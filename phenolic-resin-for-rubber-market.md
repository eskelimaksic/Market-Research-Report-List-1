<p><p>Phenolic resin for rubber market encompasses three primary types: tackifying resin, reinforcing resin, and adhesive resin. </p><p>Tackifying resin enhances the stickiness and bond strength of rubber products, improving adhesion in applications like tires and sealants. Reinforcing resin provides structural support, increasing durability and tensile strength in rubber compounds. Adhesive resin is used for bonding rubber to various surfaces, ensuring strong, long-lasting connections in manufacturing and assembly processes. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing rubber performance and applications.</p></p>
