<p><p>The lead oxide sputtering target market consists of two primary types: rotatable transformation and non-rotating type. Rotatable transformation targets feature a cylindrical design that allows for continuous rotation during the sputtering process, enabling uniform material deposition and improved efficiency. In contrast, non-rotating type targets remain stationary, which can limit deposition consistency but may be suitable for specific applications. Both types are essential in industries like electronics and optoelectronics, where thin film coatings are critical for device performance.</p></p>
