<p><p>The muscle stimulator market encompasses various device types targeting specific neurological and muscular conditions. Spinal cord stimulation (SCS) devices manage chronic pain by delivering impulses to the spinal cord. Deep brain stimulation (DBS) devices help treat movement disorders like Parkinson's disease. Vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) devices are used for epilepsy and depression. Sacral nerve stimulation (SNS) manages urinary and bowel disorders, while gastric electric stimulation (GES) aids weight loss. Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) provides pain relief through skin electrodes, and neuromuscular electrical stimulation (NMES/EMS) enhances muscle function and recovery. Other emerging technologies continue to expand this market.</p></p>
