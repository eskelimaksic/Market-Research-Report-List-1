<p><strong>The Portable Interactive Whiteboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Projection</li><li>Rear Projection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Interactive Whiteboard market is divided into two main types: front projection and rear projection. Front projection whiteboards use a projector placed in front of the board, casting images onto its surface, which can lead to shadows from users. In contrast, rear projection systems place the projector behind the board, eliminating shadows and creating a clearer viewing experience. Both types are designed for mobile use in educational and corporate settings, enhancing interactivity and engagement during presentations and collaborative sessions.</p></p>
