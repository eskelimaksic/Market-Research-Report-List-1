<p><p>The dog potty training attractant market can be segmented into two main types: spay and drip. Spay attractants are designed for targeted application, using a spray bottle to apply the product directly where the dog is encouraged to relieve itself. This method allows for precise usage and is often seen as more convenient. Drip attractants, on the other hand, are typically used in pads or mats, allowing the product to be gradually released to draw the dog to the appropriate spot. Both types aim to facilitate effective potty training.</p></p>
