<p><strong>The Gas Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerostatic</li><li>Aerodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas bearing market encompasses various types, primarily aerostatic and aerodynamic bearings. Aerostatic bearings rely on a steady supply of pressurized gas to create a stable film that supports the load, offering high precision and minimal friction, ideal for applications requiring constant speed. In contrast, aerodynamic bearings utilize the motion of the shaft to generate a pressure differential in the gas film, providing dynamic support that enhances efficiency at higher speeds. Both types improve performance, reduce wear, and extend equipment lifespan.</p></p>
