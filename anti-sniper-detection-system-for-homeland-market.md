<p><p>Anti-sniper detection systems are critical technologies for enhancing homeland security. They utilize various market types, including infrared, laser, and acoustic detection. Infrared systems detect the heat signature of a sniper's rifle, allowing for rapid localization. Laser systems employ beams to pinpoint sources of light used in aiming, providing precise tracking. Acoustic systems analyze sound waves generated by gunfire, enabling identification of sniper locations through triangulation. Collectively, these technologies enhance situational awareness and response capabilities in urban and sensitive environments.</p></p>
