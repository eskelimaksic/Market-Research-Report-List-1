<p><strong>The Electronic Board Level Underfill and Encapsulation Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Capillary Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li><li>Wafer level Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic board level underfill and encapsulation material market encompasses various types of materials used to protect and enhance the reliability of electronic assemblies. No flow underfill offers high viscosity, preventing movement during curing, while capillary underfill utilizes capillary action to fill gaps between components. Molded underfill involves the application of a rigid mold to form a protective layer around components. Wafer-level underfill is applied directly to wafers before dicing, streamlining the assembly process and improving efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications and requirements in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
