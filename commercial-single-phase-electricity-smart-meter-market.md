<p><ul><li>IC Card</li><li>Non-IC Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Single Phase Electricity Smart Meter market is categorized into two types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit technology, allowing users to load credits onto a card, which is then inserted into the meter for energy access. Non-IC Card meters, on the other hand, do not require physical cards, often utilizing prepaid or postpaid billing systems through digital payments. Both types aim to enhance energy management and streamline billing processes for commercial users.</p></p>
