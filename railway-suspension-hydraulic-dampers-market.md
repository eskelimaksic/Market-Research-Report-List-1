<p><ul><li>Primary & Secondary Suspension (Horizontal & Vertical)</li><li>Yaw Dampers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway suspension hydraulic dampers are crucial components in enhancing ride quality and stability. Primary suspension dampers, positioned between the wheelset and the train body, absorb vertical shocks, ensuring smooth travel. They can operate in both horizontal and vertical planes to manage forces effectively. Secondary suspension dampers, located between the car body and bogie, focus on lateral and vertical stability, reducing vibrations. Yaw dampers limit lateral movement, enhancing track stability and improving overall safety in railway operations.</p></p>
