<p><p>The military infrared thermal imager market comprises two primary types: uncooled infrared thermal imagers and refrigeration infrared thermal imagers. Uncooled imagers operate without the need for cooling systems, making them more compact, lightweight, and suitable for various military applications, including handheld devices. In contrast, refrigeration infrared thermal imagers utilize cooling mechanisms, offering superior sensitivity and resolution, ideal for high-performance applications such as surveillance and reconnaissance missions. Both types enhance situational awareness and target acquisition in diverse military environments.</p></p>
