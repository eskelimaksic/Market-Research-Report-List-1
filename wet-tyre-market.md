<p><p>The wet tire market consists of specialized tires designed for various wet conditions. Full wet tires feature deep grooves and soft compounds for maximum grip in heavy rain, ensuring optimal performance on aquaplaning-prone surfaces. Intermediate tires bridge the gap between full wet and dry conditions, offering flexibility for damp tracks with light rain. Wet weather tires, a broader category, encompass both full wet and intermediate options, providing enhanced traction and safety in varying moisture levels on racetracks.</p></p>
