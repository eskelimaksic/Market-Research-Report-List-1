<p><ul><li>Beat frequency oscillations</li><li>Pulse induction</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoe metal detector market is primarily categorized into three types: beat frequency oscillation (BFO), pulse induction (PI), and others. Beat frequency oscillation detectors operate by comparing two frequencies to identify metal, making them sensitive to small objects. Pulse induction detectors send short bursts of energy and are effective in challenging environments, providing deeper penetration. Other types may encompass advanced technologies or hybrid systems that blend features of BFO and PI, catering to specialized applications in security and detection.</p></p>
