<p><p>The implantable contact lens market encompasses three primary types tailored to specific vision issues. Myopia contact lenses are designed to correct nearsightedness, allowing patients to see distant objects clearly. Hyperopia contact lenses address farsightedness, enabling clarity for close-up tasks. Astigmatism contact lenses correct distorted or blurred vision caused by irregular eye curvature. Each type aims to enhance visual acuity and improve quality of life for patients, fostering advancements in eye care technology and treatment options.</p></p>
