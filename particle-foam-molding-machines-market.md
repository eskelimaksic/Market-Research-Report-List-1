<p><p>Particle foam molding machines are categorized into manual and automatic types based on operation methodology. Manual machines require human intervention for loading, processing, and unloading materials, offering flexibility but often lower output rates. In contrast, automatic machines streamline the production process with minimal operator involvement, increasing efficiency and consistency in output. As industry demands rise, automatic systems are gaining popularity due to their capacity for high-volume production, while manual machines remain suitable for smaller-scale operations or custom projects.</p></p>
