<p><strong>The Steel Abrasive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Shot</li><li>Steel Grit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel abrasive market is primarily divided into two key types: steel shot and steel grit. Steel shot consists of spherical particles used for peening and surface preparation, offering a smooth finish and enhanced durability. Steel grit, on the other hand, features angular particles that provide aggressive cutting action, making it ideal for more demanding applications like rust removal and surface profiling. Both types are essential for various industrial processes, including metalworking and construction, ensuring efficiency and quality in surface treatment.</p></p>
