<p><strong>The Fire Retardant Plywood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UCFA</li><li>UCFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire retardant plywood is classified into two main market types: UCFA (Uncoated Fire-Resistant Adhesive) and UCFB (Uncoated Fire-Resistant Board). UCFA plywood is treated with fire-retardant chemicals that enhance its resistance to ignition and flame spread without protective coatings. In contrast, UCFB plywood is designed specifically for structural applications, offering enhanced fire resistance while retaining structural integrity. Both types are used in construction, furniture, and other applications where fire safety is a critical concern, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
