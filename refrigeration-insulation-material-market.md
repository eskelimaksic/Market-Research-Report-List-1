<p><p>The refrigeration insulation material market encompasses several key types, each offering unique benefits. Elastomeric foam is flexible and moisture-resistant, ideal for pipe insulation. Polyurethane (PU) and polyisocyanurate (PIR) foams provide excellent thermal resistance. Polystyrene foam is lightweight and cost-effective, widely used in various applications. Fiberglass insulation is known for its fire resistance and thermal performance. Phenolic foam offers low thermal conductivity and superior fire performance. Other materials include cellulose and mineral wool, catering to diverse insulation needs in refrigeration.</p></p>
