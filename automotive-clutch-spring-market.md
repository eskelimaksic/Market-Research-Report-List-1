<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Spring</li><li>Central Spring</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch spring market primarily comprises three types: diaphragm springs, central springs, and others. Diaphragm springs offer lightweight and compact designs, providing efficient disengagement and engagement of the clutch. Central springs, often used in traditional clutch systems, offer a more linear force distribution, enhancing reliability in power transmission. Other springs may include coil springs or specialized designs for specific vehicle models, catering to diverse performance and durability requirements within the automotive sector. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing clutch performance.</p></p>
