<p><ul><li>Maglev Pumps</li><li>Diaphragm Pumps</li><li>Bellows Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wet process pumps market includes three primary types: Maglev pumps, diaphragm pumps, and bellows pumps. Maglev pumps utilize magnetic levitation technology for contact-free operation, ensuring minimal contamination and high reliability. Diaphragm pumps offer precise fluid handling and are suitable for corrosive chemicals, providing efficient sealing. Bellows pumps, known for their robust construction, deliver accurate flow control and are ideal for ultra-pure applications. Together, these pumps play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes, emphasizing cleanliness and precision.</p></p>
