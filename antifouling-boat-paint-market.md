<p><strong>The Antifouling Boat Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Antifouling</li><li>Eroding Antifouling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifouling boat paint market comprises two primary types: hard antifouling and eroding antifouling paints. Hard antifouling paints form a durable coating that provides long-lasting protection against marine organisms, making them suitable for vessels frequently in use. Eroding antifouling paints, on the other hand, gradually wear away over time, releasing biocides to prevent fouling. These are ideal for boats that are less frequently used, as they offer effective protection without requiring heavy maintenance. Both types cater to various boating needs.</p></p>
