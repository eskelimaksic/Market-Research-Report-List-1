<p><p>The 5G electromagnetic wave shield film market comprises two primary types: metal-mesh films and coating films. Metal-mesh films utilize finely woven metallic patterns to effectively block high-frequency electromagnetic waves, offering excellent shielding while maintaining transparency. Coating films, on the other hand, apply specialized materials that absorb or reflect electromagnetic radiation, enhancing device safety and signal integrity. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring compatible and efficient 5G communications while minimizing potential interference, catering to diverse technological applications.</p></p>
