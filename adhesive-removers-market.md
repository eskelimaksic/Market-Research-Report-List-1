<p><strong>The Adhesive Removers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Citrus Based</li><li>Soy Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive removers are classified into three primary types: citrus-based, soy-based, and solvent-based. Citrus-based removers utilize natural solvents derived from citrus fruits, providing a non-toxic and pleasant-smelling option ideal for light adhesive tasks. Soy-based removers rely on soy protein, offering an eco-friendly alternative that is effective for various adhesives. Solvent-based removers contain harsher chemical solvents, making them powerful for tough tasks, but can emit strong odors and may require more safety precautions during use. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and application needs.</p></p>
