<p><p>The overhead conveyor system market is categorized into three primary types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual systems. Automatic systems operate independently, using sensors and control mechanisms to transport materials without human intervention, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. Semi-automatic systems require some human input, offering a balance between automation and manual operation, which allows for flexibility in various workflows. Manual systems rely entirely on human effort, suited for smaller operations or specialized tasks where automation isn’t feasible.</p></p>
