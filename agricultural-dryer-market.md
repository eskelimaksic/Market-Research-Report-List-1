<p><p>The agricultural dryer market is categorized into mobile and stationary dryers. Mobile dryers are designed for flexibility, enabling farmers to dry crops on-site, reducing transportation costs and time. They are often equipped with wheels and can be easily relocated. In contrast, stationary dryers are fixed installations usually found at processing facilities or farms, optimized for large-scale operations. They typically handle higher volumes and offer more advanced drying technology, making them suitable for commercial agricultural production.</p></p>
