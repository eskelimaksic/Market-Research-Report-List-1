<p><strong>The Drilling Mud Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling mud additives market comprises two primary types: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based muds utilize oil as the continuous phase, offering excellent lubrication and thermal stability, often suitable for high-temperature wells. They are effective in controlling fluid loss and managing wellbore stability. Conversely, water-based muds use water as the continuous phase and are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective. They provide adequate cooling and cleaning but may require additional additives to enhance performance in challenging conditions.</p></p>
