<p><p>The HTCC (High-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic) substrates market comprises primarily two types: Al2O3 HTCC substrates and AIN HTCC substrates. Al2O3 (Aluminum Oxide) substrates offer excellent insulating properties and thermal stability, making them ideal for various electronic applications. On the other hand, AIN (Aluminum Nitride) substrates provide superior thermal conductivity and are increasingly preferred in high-power and high-frequency applications. Both types are essential in advancing modern electronics, including RF devices, power modules, and sensors.</p></p>
