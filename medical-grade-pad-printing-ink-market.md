<p><strong>The Medical Grade Pad Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade pad printing ink market encompasses two primary types: solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents to facilitate adhesion and drying, offering flexibility and a broad color range, but may pose environmental concerns. In contrast, UV curing inks rely on ultraviolet light to quickly solidify the ink on substrates, providing excellent durability and resistance to chemicals. Both types cater to the stringent hygiene standards required in medical applications, ensuring safety and reliability in printed components.</p></p>
