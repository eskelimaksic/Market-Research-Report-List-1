<p><p>The mobile air purifier market features several types: </p><p>1. **HEPA** filters trap small particles like dust and allergens, providing high-efficiency filtration. </p><p>2. **Activated Carbon** filters eliminate odors and volatile organic compounds through adsorption. </p><p>3. **Static Electricity** units use electrostatic attraction to capture pollutants. </p><p>4. **Ion and Ozone Generators** release ions to clump airborne particles, though ozone generation can raise health concerns. </p><p>5. **Others** include technologies like UV light or photocatalytic systems, contributing to diverse air cleaning solutions tailored to various needs.</p></p>
