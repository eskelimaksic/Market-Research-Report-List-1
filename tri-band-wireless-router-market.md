<p><strong>The Tri-band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tri-band wireless router market encompasses various Wi-Fi standards, including 802.11a, 802.11b, 802.11g, 802.11n, and 802.11ac. Each standard offers different speeds and frequencies, impacting performance. 802.11a operates at 5 GHz with speed up to 54 Mbps, while 802.11b uses 2.4 GHz with speeds up to 11 Mbps. 802.11g combines both frequencies at 54 Mbps, and 802.11n improves range and speed, supporting up to 600 Mbps. 802.11ac further enhances performance, focusing on high-speed connections and multi-device handling.</p></p>
